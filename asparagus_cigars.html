Asparagus Puff Pastry Cigars

Puff Pastry Cigars make a cute and delicious finger food appetizer for your next party or game night!

Ingredients

  • 5oz Puff Pastry
  • 1oz Crean Cheese
  • Zest from 1 Lemon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 10 Apsaragus Spears
  • 1 Egg Beaten
  • 2 Tabelspoons Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 400Â°F
  2. Blend cream cheese with lemon zest
  3. Roll the puff pastry out on a floured surface into a 6in x 10in rectangle and spread the cream cheese over.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Using a sharp knife, cut the pastry into 10 long, thin strips
  6. Wrap one pastry strip in a spiral around each asparagus spear and place onto a baking tray
  7. Lightly brush each with beaten egg and sprinkle the cheese on top
  8. Bake the cigars in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the pastry has risen and is golden-brown
